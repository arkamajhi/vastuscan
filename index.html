About Us

We’re Arka Majhi and Bawesh Pradhan—Architects, technologists, and believers in blending the ancient wisdom of Vastu Shastra with the power of AI, Extended Reality (XR), and Spatial Intelligence.

Graduates of the School of Planning and Architecture, New Delhi (SPA Delhi)—India’s most prestigious Centre of Excellence for Architecture—we are both Council of Architecture (CoA) registered architects with years of hands-on design experience and a shared curiosity for next-gen tech.

VastuScan was born from a simple yet radical idea:
What if your home could talk back through data?
What if harmony could be visualized—before a brick was laid?

With a background in architectural computation, and AI-integrated spatial analysis, we’ve built a platform that goes beyond the checklist-based approach of traditional Vastu consultation.

VastuScan is our way of making spiritual spatial intelligence accessible to everyone—homeowners, realtors, and builders alike—through 3D scans, interactive VR walkthroughs, and actionable insights. It is powered by a proprietary AI engine, VastuAI that analyzes floor plans and space-scans to deliver annotated insights, VR walkthroughs, and design-level remedies—instantly and interactively.
